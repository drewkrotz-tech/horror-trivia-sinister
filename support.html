Common Questions

My score didn't save to the leaderboard.
Leaderboard submissions require an internet connection. If you were offline when you finished a round, your score won't sync. Make sure you're connected and try a fresh round.

I found a question with a wrong answer.
Let me know — include the question text and the correct answer if you can. I'll fix it in the next content update.

Can I suggest questions or categories?
Absolutely. Email me with your ideas. I add new questions regularly and genre suggestions from actual horror fans are gold.

The app crashed or is stuck.
Try force-quitting and reopening first. If that doesn't fix it, email me with your iPhone model and iOS version and I'll dig in.
